# T1.4 SFT 热身数据生成

## 基本信息
- **优先级**: P0
- **预计耗时**: 1 天
- **依赖**: T1.1 (需要真实问题作为输入)
- **产出**: `data/sft_warmup.jsonl` (200 条)
- **预计 API 成本**: ~$2-3 (GPT-4o 生成)
- **状态**: [ ] 未开始

## 目标
生成 200 条高质量 SFT 热身样本，覆盖全部 6 种 Agent 类型 × 3 种任务类型 × 多种复杂度路径，确保 GRPO 训练前模型已掌握 `<call>` 标签格式。

## 为什么是 200 条（而非 50-100）
- A100 算力充裕，SFT 训练成本极低（~30min）
- 更多样本 = 更好的格式学习 + 更稳定的 GRPO 起点
- 需要覆盖 6 种 agent × 3 种任务 × 多种路径组合 = 最少需要 ~150 条才能全覆盖
- AgentConductor 用了 4500 条 SFT，200 条仍然很克制

## 覆盖矩阵

| 路径模式 | 涉及 Agent | 示例任务 | 目标数量 |
|----------|-----------|----------|----------|
| think → executor_cheap → answer | executor_cheap | 简单事实 QA | 40 |
| think → refiner → executor_cheap → answer | refiner, executor_cheap | 模糊描述 QA | 25 |
| think → executor_strong → answer | executor_strong | 需深度推理 | 20 |
| think → decomposer → executor×2 → synthesizer → answer | decomposer, executor, synthesizer | 多跳推理 | 35 |
| think → decomposer → executor_strong×2 → critic → answer | decomposer, executor_strong, critic | 复杂推理 | 30 |
| think → decomposer → executor_strong → critic → executor_strong → answer | 全部 | 复杂+验证+重试 | 20 |
| think → executor_cheap → answer (代码) | executor_cheap | 简单代码 | 15 |
| think → decomposer → executor_strong → critic → answer (代码) | decomposer, executor_strong, critic | 复杂代码 | 15 |
| **总计** | | | **200** |

## 数据格式

```jsonl
{
  "input": "When did World War 2 end?",
  "output": "<think>This is a simple factual question. I can use executor_cheap to get a direct answer quickly.</think>\n<call type=\"executor_cheap\">When did World War 2 end?</call>\n<information>World War 2 ended on September 2, 1945, when Japan formally surrendered aboard the USS Missouri.</information>\n<answer>World War 2 ended on September 2, 1945.</answer>"
}
```

```jsonl
{
  "input": "Who was the president of the US when the Berlin Wall fell, and what party did they belong to?",
  "output": "<think>This is a multi-hop question requiring: (1) when the Berlin Wall fell, (2) who was US president then, (3) their party. I should decompose it.</think>\n<call type=\"decomposer\">Break down: Who was the president of the US when the Berlin Wall fell, and what party did they belong to?</call>\n<information>Sub-tasks:\n1. When did the Berlin Wall fall?\n2. Who was the US president at that time?\n3. What political party did they belong to?</information>\n<call type=\"executor_cheap\">When did the Berlin Wall fall and who was the US president at that time?</call>\n<information>The Berlin Wall fell on November 9, 1989. George H.W. Bush was the US president at that time.</information>\n<call type=\"synthesizer\">Combine: The Berlin Wall fell on November 9, 1989. George H.W. Bush was the US president. What party did he belong to?</call>\n<information>George H.W. Bush was the 41st President of the United States, serving from 1989 to 1993. He was a member of the Republican Party.</information>\n<answer>George H.W. Bush was the president when the Berlin Wall fell, and he belonged to the Republican Party.</answer>"
}
```

  - 格式要求（必须有 `<think>`, `<call>`, `<information>`, `<answer>`）
- 添加覆盖矩阵控制：确保每种路径模式生成指定数量

## 注意事项
- `<information>` 块是模拟的（GPT-4o 想象 agent 会返回什么），训练时不需要真实 API 返回
- 代码任务的 SFT 示例需要特别注意：`<call type="executor_strong">` 应包含完整代码描述
- 如果 GPT-4o 生成质量不稳定，考虑增加 retry + 格式过滤
