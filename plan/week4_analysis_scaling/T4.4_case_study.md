## Case Study 1: 错误恢复 (Reactive Recovery)

**场景**: executor 返回了错误/低质量信息，orchestrator 临时调用 critic 验证后重试。

```xml
Input: "Who was the youngest Nobel Prize winner in Physics?"

<think>This is a factual question, I'll use executor_cheap for efficiency.</think>
<call type="executor_cheap">Who was the youngest Nobel Prize winner in Physics?</call>
<information>The youngest Nobel Prize winner was Malala Yousafzai who won in 2014.</information>

<!-- Orchestrator 发现信息可能有误 (Malala 获的是和平奖不是物理奖) -->
<think>The response mentions Malala, but she won the Peace Prize, not Physics.
I need to verify and get the correct answer.</think>
<call type="critic">Verify: Is Malala Yousafzai the youngest Nobel Prize winner in Physics?
The answer seems incorrect as she won the Peace Prize.</call>
<information>Incorrect. Malala won the Nobel Peace Prize. The youngest Physics laureate
is Lawrence Bragg, who won in 1915 at age 25.</information>

<answer>Lawrence Bragg, who won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1915 at age 25.</answer>
```

**对比 Non-Reactive**: 无法发现 executor 的错误，直接输出 "Malala"。

## Case Study 2: 简单题走捷径 (Efficient Shortcut)

**场景**: 简单事实题，orchestrator 跳过 decomposer/critic 直接 1 步解决。

```xml
Input: "What is the chemical symbol for gold?"

<think>Simple factual question. executor_cheap can handle this directly.</think>
<call type="executor_cheap">What is the chemical symbol for gold?</call>
<information>The chemical symbol for gold is Au, from the Latin word "aurum".</information>
<answer>Au</answer>
```

**Total: 1 turn, cost ≈ $0.0001**

**对比 Fixed-Pipeline**: 强制走 refiner → decomposer → executor → critic → synthesizer = 5 turns, cost ≈ $0.005 (50x)

## Case Study 3: 复杂多跳分解 (Complex Decomposition)

**场景**: 多跳推理题，orchestrator 自动分解 → 逐步执行 → 综合。

```xml
Input: "What is the population of the country where the director of Inception was born?"

<think>Multi-hop question:
1. Who directed Inception?
2. Where was this person born?
3. What is the population of that country?
I need to decompose this.</think>

<call type="decomposer">Break down: What is the population of the country
where the director of Inception was born?</call>
<information>Sub-tasks:
1. Who directed Inception?
2. Where was the director born?
3. What is the population of that country?</information>

<call type="executor_cheap">Who directed the movie Inception?</call>
<information>Inception was directed by Christopher Nolan.</information>

<call type="executor_cheap">Where was Christopher Nolan born?</call>
<information>Christopher Nolan was born in London, England, United Kingdom.</information>

<call type="executor_cheap">What is the population of the United Kingdom?</call>
<information>The population of the United Kingdom is approximately 67.7 million (2024).</information>

<call type="synthesizer">Combine: Director of Inception = Christopher Nolan,
born in UK, UK population = 67.7 million</call>
<information>Christopher Nolan directed Inception. He was born in London, United Kingdom,
which has a population of approximately 67.7 million.</information>

<answer>Approximately 67.7 million</answer>
```

**Total: 5 turns, cost ≈ $0.003**

## 如何获取真实案例

```bash
# 在 GRPO checkpoint 上运行指定问题
python inference/infer.py \
    --model_path checkpoints/orch_grpo_7b_seed1/final \
    --input "Who was the youngest Nobel Prize winner in Physics?" \
    --verbose  # 打印完整中间过程

# 批量跑一组候选问题，筛选有趣案例
python eval/eval_orchestrator.py \
    --model_path checkpoints/orch_grpo_7b_seed1/final \
    --data_path data/test_qa.jsonl \
    --max_samples 200 \
    --save_traces  # 保存完整轨迹
```

### 筛选标准
1. **错误恢复**: 找 agent_calls 中有 critic 且最终答案正确的样本
2. **简单捷径**: 找 turns=1 且 EM=1 的样本
3. **复杂分解**: 找 turns≥3 且有 decomposer+synthesizer 且 F1>0.8 的样本
